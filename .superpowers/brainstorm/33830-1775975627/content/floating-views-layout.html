The world map background

  • Not just decoration: The map can highlight the current location, show discovered areas, and display travel routes between regions.
  • Era indicators on map: The map itself could subtly shift — father's era shows the map with old names and intact borders, son's era shows renamed places and changed territories.
  • Switch animation: When toggling active era, the views can swap sizes with the map rippling between them.
  • 10% remaining space (~50% + ~40% = ~90%) is enough for the map to breathe and show location context without feeling wasted.

# Maren

## Overview

Aric's childhood friend and Thornwall's best tracker. Maren is a wiry, freckled young woman who can read animal trails the way Aldric reads star charts. She is practical, blunt, and fiercely protective of the people she cares about. Unlike Aric, she has no interest in Kaelen's legacy — she cares about the here and now, and the here and now is that the forest is dying and the village needs help. She joins Aric not because of destiny or duty but because someone has to keep him from getting killed.

## Backstory

Maren's parents were foresters who managed the woodlands north of Thornwall. She grew up in the canopy, learning to track, trap, and navigate by moss patterns and bird calls. When the forest began changing — trees rotting from the inside, animal migrations shifting — her parents were among the first to notice. Her father died investigating a sinkhole that opened in the deep woods when Maren was seventeen. Her mother now runs the village's dwindling herb supply. Maren took over forest patrols alone, mapping the decay and warning Thornwall's council, who largely ignored her reports until creatures began emerging from the cracks.

## Personality & Motivation

Direct to the point of bluntness, Maren has no patience for abstractions or philosophical debates. She communicates through action and expects the same from others. She is not unkind — she shows affection through practical gestures: mending gear, sharing rations, covering someone's blind spot in a fight. Her primary motivation is protecting Thornwall's remaining woodlands and her mother. Her deeper motivation is proving that the people who ignored her warnings were wrong — and making sure no one else dies the way her father did.

## Relationships

- [[Aric]] — childhood friend, the only person who took her forest reports seriously
- [[Thornwall]] — home, the village she patrols for

# Kaelen

## Overview

The Father. A wandering swordsman who became a legendary hero through a combination of genuine courage and lucky timing. Kaelen travels the land resolving crises — clearing monster nests, mediating faction disputes, protecting trade routes. The world remembers him as a paragon. The truth is more complicated: Kaelen is impulsive, self-doubting, and terrified of the growing darkness he cannot name. He left his infant son Aric in the care of Thornwall's elder to pursue what he believes is a threat too dangerous to face with a family at his back.

## Backstory

Kaelen grew up in Thornwall, a fortified farming village at the foot of the Iron Peaks. His father was a militia guard; his mother was a traveling herbalist who rarely came home. He learned swordsmanship from the village militia and survival from the forest rangers. At nineteen, he drove off a band of raiders attacking Thornwall's granary — his first act of heroism, though he later admitted he was simply too scared to run. Word spread, and over the next decade he became the region's go-to problem-solver. He married a healer named Lira, had a son (Aric), and tried to settle down. But strange tremors from the sealed caverns beneath the Iron Peaks convinced him something ancient was stirring. He left Thornwall when Aric was two years old, promising to return within a year. He never did.

## Personality & Motivation

Kaelen is warm, direct, and physically brave — the kind of person who enters a burning building without thinking. He is also emotionally avoidant: he jokes when he should grieve, fights when he should talk, and leaves when he should stay. His core motivation is protecting people from a threat he doesn't fully understand. His deeper, unspoken motivation is proving he's worthy of the legend others have built around him. He fears that if he stops moving, the gap between who he is and who people think he is will become visible.

### portrait

16-bit pixel art, grayscale base with palette-swap tinting. Man in his early thirties, strong jaw, short brown hair with a few premature gray streaks. Wears practical traveling armor — leather over chainmail, a worn green cloak fastened with a simple iron clasp. A broadsword strapped to his back. Expression is confident but weary — the look of someone who has been walking for a long time. Warm golden campfire lighting from below, casting upward shadows. Scars on his left forearm visible.

### voice

Mid-range tenor with a rough, outdoor quality. Speaks quickly when excited, slows down when trying to sound authoritative. Laughs easily — a genuine, disarming laugh that makes people trust him. When lying or deflecting, his pitch rises slightly. Regional accent: slightly clipped consonants, open vowels, the speech patterns of a farming village. Swears casually and without malice.

### theme-music

Acoustic guitar melody in G major, fingerpicked with a walking rhythm. Bright and forward-moving, suggesting open roads and purpose. A tin whistle joins in the chorus, adding a folk quality. Underneath, a low drone on a hurdy-gurdy provides grounding weight. The piece has momentum — it sounds like someone who can't sit still. Key modulates to E minor in the bridge, hinting at the cost of always moving forward.

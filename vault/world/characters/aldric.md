# Elder Aldric

## Overview

The last Starweaver of the Academy of Starlight, Aldric is a stooped, white-haired scholar who has spent sixty years deciphering the celestial charts left by the Academy's founders. He serves as Sera's mentor and the Academy's reluctant leader. Behind his gentle demeanor lies a man haunted by a catastrophic mistake made in his youth — one that sealed an entire wing of the Academy and cost the lives of three fellow scholars.

## Backstory

Aldric was born to farmers on the outskirts of the Academy's cliff. At seven, he showed an unusual ability to read starlight patterns with his bare eyes — a trait the Starweavers hadn't seen in generations. He was taken in as an apprentice and rose quickly through the ranks. At thirty-two, he attempted a ritual to open the Sealed Observatory, believing the founders had left their greatest knowledge there. The ritual went catastrophically wrong: three colleagues died, the west wing collapsed, and the Observatory remained sealed. Aldric never attempted advanced star-reading again. He retreated into teaching and administrative duties, channeling his guilt into protecting students from the same ambition that destroyed his friends.

## Personality & Motivation

Patient to a fault, Aldric speaks in measured tones and prefers questions over answers. He deflects direct confrontation with gentle redirection. Beneath this calm surface, he is terrified that Sera — his most gifted student in decades — will repeat his mistake. His primary motivation is protecting the Academy and its students, even at the cost of suppressing knowledge that could be dangerous. He hoards certain texts, locks certain doors, and steers students away from the Sealed Observatory. He knows this makes him a hypocrite.

## Relationships

- [[Sera]] — his most talented student, reminds him of his younger self
- [[Starlight Academy]] — he leads it reluctantly, feeling unworthy
- [[Kaelen]] — the father, who once visited the Academy seeking star-chart guidance for a distant expedition
